### `--ci-on-the-fly`

<br />

可选：允许在 CI 环境中创建[即时项目][on the fly projects]。
如果 `CI` 环境变量设置为 `true`，则为必需。
[GitHub Actions][github actions] 和 [GitLab CI/CD][gitlab ci] 都默认设置了此变量。

与[`--project` 选项][project option]冲突。

[project option]: #--project-project

[on the fly projects]: /zh/docs/how-to/claim/#on-the-fly-projects

[github actions]: https://docs.github.com/zh/actions/reference/workflows-and-actions/variables#default-environment-variables
[gitlab ci]: https://docs.gitlab.com/ci/variables/predefined_variables/#predefined-variables
