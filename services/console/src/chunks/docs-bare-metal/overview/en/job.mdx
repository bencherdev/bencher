## Job

A Job tracks the lifecycle of a remote benchmark execution request.
A custom timeout can be requested per Job with the `--job-timeout` option.
However, the following Job timeouts are always enforced:

| [Tier][pricing] | Job Timeout                     |
| --------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Unclaimed       | 1 minute                        |
| Free            | 5 minutes                       |
| Team            | Unlimited (default: 60 minutes) |
| Enterprise      | Unlimited (default: 60 minutes) |

See [the Job documentation][job] for a full overview.
