## Track your Bare Metal Benchmarks

You are now ready to track your benchmark results on bare metal hardware!
To do so, you will use [the `bencher run` CLI subcommand][bencher run]
to run your benchmarks and collect the results.
Replace `YOUR_PROJECT_SLUG` with the project slug from the previous steps and run:

<TerminalCommand code="bencher run --project YOUR_PROJECT_SLUG --image YOUR_PROJECT_SLUG:latest --spec intel-v1 bencher noise --format json" />

We are using [the `bencher noise` subcommand][bencher noise] as our benchmark command to generate some sample data.
It measures how noisy your benchmark environment is.
When we use [the `--format json` option][format option] the results are output in [Bencher Metric Format (BMF)][bmf].
