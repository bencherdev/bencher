## Job

A Job tracks the lifecycle of a remote benchmark execution request.
The Job must specify the [benchmark command][benchmark command] and which [Spec][spec] it wants to use.
The Job is then assigned to a [Runner][runner] that supports the specified Spec.
See [Bare Metal images][image] for a full overview.
